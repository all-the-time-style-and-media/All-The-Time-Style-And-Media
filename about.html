ABOUT ME


Hello!

I'm Leah Z Osborn, an outdoor crazy, Jesus-devoted, dog-loving entrepreneur who wants to spread excitement for God and use my gifts to bless others!

I majoring in Management: Organizational Leadership and Interactive Digital Studies at the University of Northern Iowa and work closely with those in the Cedar Falls area. One of my favorite quotes has always been, "God is good all the time and all the time God is good." This quote is not only a great reminder of the omnipresent Creator, but also inspired All The Time Style And Media! With this project, I hope to spread the Good News and be a positive Christian in the marketplace. On the media side, I have always loved photography. It beautifully showcases people as image-bearers and the intricateness of nature. On the style side, I believe wearing Christian clothing can be a great conversation starter. I love doing graphic design work and hope that it will be used to plant seeds for future disciples. I would love for you to join this new entrepreneurial journey with me!

Let's go, God!
